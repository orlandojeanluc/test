Investment Approach

1. Diversity of Model

Relying on a single model or fundamental outlook can expose you to unnecessary risk. At Caltropia Research, our approach is different—every model is built to incorporate multiple signals and perspectives, reducing reliance on any one view and helping to manage risk more effectively.

2. Diversity of Strategy

Depending on a single strategy or model portfolio to manage risk can actually increase your exposure to left-tail events and amplify the potential for outsized losses.

3. Diversity of Time

To avoid overexposure to any single trend or time frame, true diversification requires spreading risk across multiple time horizons. Relying too heavily on one time frame can compromise portfolio balance and increase overall risk.

4. Diversity Across and Within

Asset weighting plays a critical role in enhancing risk-adjusted returns. This doesn’t mean holding 5,000 stocks or a basket of 20 ETFs—excessive diversification can dilute performance without meaningfully reducing risk.

5. Systemically Applied

Discipline is essential to successful investing. That’s why having robust models—designed to perform across a wide range of market conditions—can be a powerful advantage in maximizing risk-adjusted returns.
