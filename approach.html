Managed Futures Trend

Signal-driven managed futures strategies using trend, strength, mean reversion, and breakout/down models.

Overview

At Caltropia Research, we offer two distinct managed futures strategies, both designed to capitalize on markets trends, strength, mean reversion, and breakout/down signals — but each optimized through a different portfolio construction approach.

Learn More Below

1: Caltropia Managed Futures Alpha.

This strategy employs a proprietary asset weighting algorithm that dynamically adjusts position sizes based on a blend of trend strength, signal reliability, and volatility normalization.

Methodology

1. Signal Inputs

Short and intermediate term trend, strength ratings, mean reversion triggers, and breakout/down events.

2. Weighting's:

Custom algorithm prioritizes assets showing stronger trend persistence, higher breakout probability, and optimal risk-adjusted characteristics.

3. Objective:

Maximize overall strategy conviction by overweighting assets with the most statistically favorable conditions while maintaining diversified exposure.

4. Rebalance:

Dynamic; adjusted based on real-time signal shifts and volatility scaling.

Who's This For?

This strategy employs a proprietary asset weighting algorithm that dynamically adjusts position sizes based on a blend of trend strength, signal reliability, and volatility normalization.

2: Caltropia Managed Futures Hybrid Equal Weight

1. Signal Inputs

Identical — trend direction, strength scores, mean reversion factors, and breakout/down confirmations.

2. Weighting's:

Primarily equal-weighted across assets passing signal thresholds, with tilts toward stronger signals when appropriate.

3. Objective:

Achieve broad diversification across asset classes while maintaining responsiveness to prevailing market trends.

4. Rebalance:

Scheduled (e.g., weekly) with interim adjustments if extreme trend reversals occur.

Who's This For?

Best suited for investors seeking broad exposure and balance, favoring diversification over heavy conviction tilts.
